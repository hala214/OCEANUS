    OCEANUS Introduction

    We are a group who wants to employ technology to protect the marine ecosystem. We created a smart marine buoy that can do a veriety of things to help save the ocean, including:

    1. Identify and protect endangered species from fishermen .

    2. Detect and avoid cable damage caused by ship anchores.

    3. Track and identify illegal ships that enter prohibited zones.

    4. It can predict the weather and ocean currents using a sensor.

    *Our marine buoy is powered by solar energy and wave currents

    As our future plans it will report illegal rubbish dumping in the water

    And our marine buoy will communicate with other buoys to track down the illegal ships, and report authorities ,sea and oceans cleaning robots, and workers

    We have a website that shows all the data from the buoys in user friendly way.

    Our project aims to use technology to preserve the marine environment for future generations .Join us in our mission to save the ocean!.

    Our mission , Our vission.

    Our mission: Accidents can happen at sea and in oceans due to things like ships crashing, getting stuck on reefs, or spilling oil. These accidents often come from mistakes, bad weather, or equipment problems. These incidents can cause harm to people, harm the environment, and cost a lot of money. It's important to be careful, use good technology, and have plans in place to handle accidents and keep the oceans safe.

    Our marine buoy project comes with aligns to solve modern problems , located in our ocean and seas affecting our technology ,ships and cables , with this project we aim to better the conditions surrounding these issues for a brighter future that supports our connection over the sea and the sea life .

    Under sea cables are vital for global communication and internet connectivity as they carry more than 95% of the worldâ€™s data traffic , however through research our team discovered that in the past years these cables were harmed to due to vulnerabilities to physical and digital threats one of the primary causes to this type of damage was by commercial fishing and shipping activities

    The importance of OCEANUS:

    1- Protect cables from damaging by detecting the location of this area ,stopping ships from entering this area by showing signals of no entry .

    2- Protect the rare species from the fishermen and alert them that this is a banned area and the fishing is not allowed.

    3- Determine the ships (the legal and illegal ships ) and sending the data of them to the website.

    4- Measures the humidity and temperature using sensors and send the data to the website .
